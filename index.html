Engagement model

A calm, data-led process that meets you where you are

We start by understanding constraints, then iterate with tight feedback loops so we can deliver meaningful outcomes quickly.

  1. Discovery intensive
    Rapid audit of architecture, product goals, and team workflows. Expect actionable insights within the first week.
  2. Co-created delivery plan
    We prioritise initiatives, clarify success metrics, and align scope with time-to-impact and budget.
  3. Execution with mentorship
    I work alongside your team, pairing on critical code paths, facilitating technical design sessions, and unblocking delivery.
  4. Outcome-focused iteration
    Weekly demos, instrumentation reviews, and retrospectives ensure we stay aligned and keep learning.
